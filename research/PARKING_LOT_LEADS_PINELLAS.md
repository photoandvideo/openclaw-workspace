## HOW TO FIND MORE LEADS

### Google Maps Method:
1. Open Google Maps
2. Search: "shopping center Clearwater FL" / "church Largo FL" / "medical office park St Pete FL"
3. Look for locations with large parking lots (visible from satellite view)
4. Click → Get phone number → Add to your list

### Best Time to Call:
- **Tuesday–Thursday: 9am–11am or 2pm–4pm**
- Avoid Mondays (busy) and Fridays (people checking out)
- Ask for "facilities manager" or "property manager"
