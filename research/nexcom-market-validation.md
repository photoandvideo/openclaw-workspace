## Competitive Landscape

**Direct competitors:**
- **Twilio Studio** — DIY, cheap, technical (plumbers won't use it)
- **Zapier + Chatbot** — expensive to assemble ($200–500/mo parts)
- **Drift** — $500+/mo, overkill for local businesses
- **HubSpot Chatbots** — $50+/mo, okay, but not SMS-focused
- **Botsonic** — $25–99/mo, generic, no local specialization

**Your competitive advantage:**
- ✅ **SMS-first** (local businesses live on their phone)
- ✅ **Specialized for service industries** (not generic B2B)
- ✅ **Done-for-you** (they don't touch the code)
- ✅ **Real human support** (you, not a chatbot)
- ✅ **Fast setup** (24–48 hours, per your docs)

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ No brand recognition (you're unknown)
- ❌ Solo founder (hard to scale quickly)
- ❌ Competitors have VC funding (but also slower, more expensive)
