## PART 1: MARKET ANALYSIS

### The Problem We Solve

**Statistic:** According to Twilio research, service businesses lose an average of **$500–2,000 per missed call**.

**Why it happens:**
- Trade workers are on jobs, can't answer phones
- Calls go to voicemail → customers call competitor
- 24/7 availability expectation (but manually impossible)

**Current solutions (all suck):**
- Hire a receptionist ($2,000–4,000/mo) — Too expensive
- Call forwarding to personal phone — Still miss calls while busy
- Generic chatbots — Don't understand trade business context
- Answering services ($500–1,500/mo) — Slow, impersonal, no automation

**The gap:** No solution that's affordable ($300–500), intelligent (AI), AND specialized for trades.

**NexcomAI fills that gap.**
