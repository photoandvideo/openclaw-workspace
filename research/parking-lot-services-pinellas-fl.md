# Market Research: Parking Lot Services — Pinellas County, FL
*Researched: 2026-04-10 | By: Finn 🦊*

---

## Executive Summary

Strong demand exists in Pinellas County for parking lot striping, resurfacing, and patching. The market is active but fragmented — dominated by a handful of established local players and national franchise operators. Entry is viable for a hungry operator who can undercut on price and hustle for smaller commercial accounts.

---

## 1. Market Demand

**Why it's strong in Pinellas:**
- High concentration of commercial properties — strip malls, plazas, restaurants, churches, HOAs, car dealerships, warehouses
- Florida's intense sun and heat degrade asphalt and paint faster than most states — restriping needed every 2–3 years minimum
- Heavy tourist traffic (beach towns: Clearwater Beach, St Pete Beach, Treasure Island) = parking lots that take serious abuse
- ADA compliance is legally required and enforced — creates constant demand for re-striping

**Demand signals found:**
- Multiple companies specifically advertising Pinellas County coverage (Clearwater, St. Pete, Largo, Pinellas Park)
- "Anyway Line Striping" (Largo, FL) — active Facebook page soliciting Pinellas area customers
- Companies like All Phase Paving (since 1971) and Anderson & Sons dominating with no shortage of work
- EverLine Coatings (franchise) recently expanded into St. Petersburg — sign of national brands seeing opportunity

## 4. What You Need to Start (Striping Focus)

**Equipment (striping only — lowest startup cost):**
- Line striping machine: $1,500 – $8,000 (Graco or Titan brand)
- Traffic paint (latex): ~$20–$30/gallon (covers ~300 sq ft)
- Stencils (ADA, arrows, etc.): $200 – $500 set
- Measuring tape, chalk line, templates

**Estimated startup cost (striping only):** $3,000 – $12,000

**Licensing in Florida:**
- General contractor license or painting contractor license may be required for commercial work
- Florida DBPR (Dept of Business & Professional Regulation) governs this
- Some cities require local business license
- Liability insurance required ($1M general liability typical)
- No special asphalt license needed for striping-only work

## 6. Verdict

**Is the demand there?** Yes — clearly. Multiple companies are doing well, national franchises are entering the market, and the recurring nature of the service (every 2–3 years) means repeat business.

**Is there room for a new player?** Yes — especially targeting:
- Small commercial accounts (under 100 spaces)
- Same-week turnaround (big players are slow)
- Competitive pricing

**Lowest risk entry point:** Striping-only. Minimal equipment, fast jobs, immediate cash flow. Add patching and sealcoating later. Resurfacing requires heavier equipment and capital — phase 3.

**Pinellas specifically:** Strong tourist economy + aging commercial real estate = constant maintenance needs. Good market.
