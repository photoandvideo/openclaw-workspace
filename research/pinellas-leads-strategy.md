## Overview

## Verified Platforms with Active Demand

### High-Priority (Customers Submit Requests for You)

**1. Thumbtack**
- **What happens:** Property managers/business owners submit jobs, Thumbtack sends you the lead
- **Your cost:** Free to join, pay per lead you respond to ($3–$5 per lead typically)
- **Why it works:** Customers actively seeking stripers right now
- **Action:** 
  - Go to thumbtack.com, sign up as "Parking Lot Striping" pro
  - Set service area: Pinellas County (Clearwater, St. Petersburg, Largo, Pinellas Park)
  - Create profile with photos of striping work (borrow from Google Images of striping if needed)
  - Enable lead notifications

**2. Angi (formerly Angie's List)**
- **What happens:** Similar to Thumbtack — customers submit requests, you get notified
- **Your cost:** Free profile, optional paid membership for more leads ($50–$100/month)
- **Why it works:** Established platform, older demographic = property managers
- **Action:**
  - Go to angieslist.com
  - Create pro account for "Parking Lot Striping" or "Asphalt Services"
  - Fill out service areas, experience level
  - Get ready for quotes

---

### Medium-Priority (You Post, Monitor, Respond Fast)

**3. Craigslist Tampa Bay — Services**
- **URL:** tampa.craigslist.org → Services → scroll to "Skilled Trades"
- **What you do:** Post your striping services, check for customer requests daily
- **Why it works:** Free, local, customers actively browsing for contractors
- **Your posting should say:**
  - "Professional Parking Lot Striping — Clearwater, St Petersburg, Largo, Pinellas"
  - $4–$8 per space or flat rates for small jobs
  - Free estimates
  - ADA compliant work
  - Phone + email for quick response
- **Action:**
  - Post every 7 days (Craigslist allows re-posting, keeps you at top)
  - Check daily for customer requests like "parking lot needs striping" or "looking for parking lot painter"
  - Response time < 4 hours wins jobs

**4. Craigslist Tampa Bay — "Wanted" Section**
- **Monitor:** tampa.craigslist.org → Wanted
- **Why:** Customers post "looking for someone to stripe my parking lot"
- **Action:** Search for "parking" OR "stripe" OR "lot" daily, respond within 2 hours

### Local/Hyper-Local (High Conversion)

**5. Nextdoor**
- **What:** Hyper-local neighborhood app; HOA boards and property managers very active
- **Why it works:** People trust "verified neighbors," less competition than online
- **Action:**
  - Create Nextdoor account, verify your address (Pinellas County)
  - Post intro: "Local parking lot striping and asphalt maintenance — serving Clearwater/St Pete area"
  - Answer any "Who recommends X?" posts about asphalt/parking lot services

**6. Facebook Groups — Pinellas Business Owners**
- **Groups to find & join:**
  - "Clearwater Business Owners" (search Facebook)
  - "St. Petersburg Small Business Group"
  - "Largo FL Business Network"
  - "Pinellas County Business Connect"
  - "Tampa Bay Commercial Property Managers"

  - DON'T spam; be helpful, be real
- **Why it works:** Business owners ask for recommendations in these groups CONSTANTLY

## Direct Outreach (Cold Calling / Email)

These are categories of businesses in Pinellas with parking lots that need maintenance:

### Target Lists to Build

**Churches (highest-margin customers):**
- Most have aging parking lots, often neglected
- Sensitive to cost, but will pay for quality ADA-compliant work
- How to find: Google "churches in Clearwater FL" OR "churches in St Petersburg FL"
- Contact: Call the main number, ask for "facilities manager" or "building manager"

**Shopping Centers & Strip Malls:**
- 100+ parking lot-dependent businesses in Pinellas
- Property managers are your customers
- How to find: Google "shopping centers Clearwater" OR "strip malls St Petersburg"
- Contact: Call the property management office (usually sign in the lot), ask for manager

**Car Dealerships:**
- Clean, striped lots are critical for sales appeal
- Contact: Call dealership, ask for "lot manager" or "facilities"

**HOA-Managed Communities:**
- Hundreds in Pinellas
- Board meetings are when striping decisions happen
- How to find: Search "HOA Clearwater FL" + "HOA St Petersburg FL"
- Contact: Board president's email (usually on HOA website)

**Restaurants / Fast Casual (30+ seats):**
- Parking lot = curb appeal = customer first impression
- How to find: Google "restaurants in [city] FL"
- Contact: Call, ask for manager

**Self-Storage Facilities:**
- Always need maintenance
- How to find: Google "self storage Pinellas County"
- Contact: On-site manager

## What to Say When You Get a Lead

**First response (email or text):**
```
Hi [name],

Thanks for reaching out about parking lot striping. I'd love to help!

Could you tell me:
1. What's your address? (I serve Pinellas County — Clearwater, St Pete, Largo, etc.)
2. How many parking spaces?
3. What work do you need? (Re-stripe, fire lanes, ADA symbols, patching?)

I can have a free quote to you by [tomorrow/next day].

Looking forward to it!
[Your name]
[Your phone]
```

**Phone call (if they call):**
- Be friendly, not salesy
- Ask their address, size of lot, what they need
- Say: "I can come by for a free estimate [day/time]. Takes 15 minutes, no obligation."
- Close: "Can I pencil you in for [specific time]?"

**First estimate (in-person):**
- Take photos of the lot
- Measure or pace it out
- Quote them $4–$7 per space (cheaper than Anderson & Sons, faster than big companies)
- Example: 75 spaces = $300–$525. Simple job, 2–3 hours of work, ~$200 profit
- Ask: "Can I get you on the schedule for [next week]?"
