# Nexcom AI — Call Script
_For cold outreach to local trade businesses & real estate agents_

---

## 🔧 TRADE BUSINESSES (Plumbers, Electricians, HVAC, Cleaning)

### OPENING (first 10 seconds — do not pitch yet)
> "Hey, is this [Name]? Hey [Name], this is Hugo calling from Nexcom AI, I'm local here in Clearwater. Do you have like 60 seconds? I want to show you something real quick."

**If they say yes:**
→ Go to THE HOOK

**If they say "what's this about?":**
> "I help trade businesses around here set up an AI assistant that answers texts and missed calls when you're on a job. Takes 60 seconds to show you. Worth it?"

**If they say they're busy:**
> "Totally get it. Can I text you a quick demo link? You can try it in 2 minutes when you have a break — it's actually pretty cool."
→ Get their number confirmed, text the demo link, follow up next day

---

### THE HOOK
> "So real quick — you know when you're up on a roof or knee-deep in a job and your phone rings and you can't answer? That lead just went to your competitor. We fix that."

---

### THE DEMO OFFER
> "I built a live demo — it's an AI assistant set up for a plumbing company. I can text you the link right now and you can try it in 2 minutes. It answers questions, gives pricing, books appointments — all automatically. Want me to send it?"

→ Text them: **nexcomai.ai/demo**

---

### WHILE THEY'RE LOOKING
> "So basically what I do is set this up for your business — your name, your services, your pricing. When someone texts or calls and you can't answer, the AI responds instantly. You wake up in the morning and the lead's already captured. All you do is call them back."

---

### HANDLING OBJECTIONS

**"How much does it cost?"**
> "Setup is $500 and then $300 a month. Most guys make that back in the first week from one job they would've missed. I actually use it myself — if you call my number right now and I'm busy, my AI picks it up."

**"I already have voicemail."**
> "Voicemail is a dead end — 80% of people hang up without leaving a message. This actually responds to them, answers their questions, and captures their info. Big difference."

**"I'm not really a tech person."**
> "You don't need to be — I do everything. You just tell me about your business and I handle the setup. Takes 48 hours and you never touch any tech."

**"I need to think about it."**
> "Totally fair. Can I send you a one-pager with all the details? And if you want, I can set up a free 15-minute Zoom where I show you exactly what it looks like for your type of business."

**"I'm not interested."**
> "No problem at all. Can I ask — is it more the cost, or you just don't see the need right now?"
→ Listen, don't push. If cost: offer to waive setup fee for first client. If no need: thank them and move on.

---

### THE CLOSE
> "So here's what I can do — I'll set up a demo version customized for your business, totally free, so you can see exactly what your customers would experience. If you like it, we go from there. If not, no hard feelings. Sound fair?"

→ **Goal of the call:** Not to sell. To get them to say yes to a demo or a Zoom.

---

## 🏠 REAL ESTATE AGENTS

### OPENING
> "Hey [Name], this is Hugo from Nexcom AI here in Clearwater. Quick question — do you have anything that handles buyer inquiries when you're in a showing or it's 10pm on a Sunday?"

**If no:**
> "That's exactly what I build. I set up an AI assistant for agents that answers buyer texts 24/7, qualifies them by budget and beds, and books showings automatically. I have a live demo running right now — can I send you the link?"

---

### THE DEMO OFFER
> "It's a real working chatbot — live property database, listing cards, the whole thing. Takes 2 minutes to try. I'll text you the link."

→ Text them: **nexcomai.ai** (real estate demo)

---

### QUALIFY FOR ZOOM
Ask these 3 questions:
1. "How many buyer inquiries do you get per week roughly?"
2. "Are most of them coming through text/DM or phone calls?"
3. "Would you be open to a quick 15-minute Zoom this week? I'll show you what it looks like with your actual listings."

→ **Goal:** Book the Zoom. That's the close for real estate.

---

### REAL ESTATE OBJECTIONS

**"I have a CRM for that."**
> "CRMs are great for managing contacts you already have. This captures the ones you never even knew about — the 2am inquiry, the Sunday DM, the person who texted while you were in a closing. Different problem."

**"My brokerage has tools."**
> "Most brokerage tools are shared platforms — this is your own branded assistant that sounds like you, with your listings, your style. Totally separate."

**"How much?"**
> "$1,000 setup + $500/month. For an agent closing 2-3 deals a month, that's rounding error. And the first month is free if you book this week."

## 📱 FOLLOW-UP TEXT (send after call)

**After a positive call:**
> "Hey [Name], Hugo from Nexcom AI. Here's that demo I mentioned — try chatting with it: nexcomai.ai
> If you want to see a version built for [their business type], I can set up a free 15-min Zoom this week. Just reply here 👍"

**After voicemail:**
> "Hey [Name], Hugo here — left you a voicemail. Built an AI assistant that handles missed calls for [plumbers/electricians/realtors]. Live demo here: nexcomai.ai — takes 2 min to try. Happy to chat anytime."

---

## 📊 TRACKING

Keep a simple log:
- Called ✅ / No answer 📵 / Not interested ❌ / Follow up 🔄 / Demo sent 📲 / Zoom booked 📅 / Closed 🎉

**Daily goal:** 5 calls minimum. 1 demo sent = good day. 1 Zoom booked = great day.

---

## 💡 MINDSET

- You're not selling. You're offering to solve a real problem they have every day.
- Every "no" gets you closer to a "yes." It's a numbers game.
- The demo does the selling — your job is just to get them to look at it.
- You USE this product yourself. That's your superpower on every call.
