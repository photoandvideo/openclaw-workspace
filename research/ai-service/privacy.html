Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Nexcom AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates AI assistant services for businesses and individuals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services, including through SMS, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp, and our website at nexcomai.ai.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our AI assistants, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact information you provide during conversations
  • Your phone number (when you text our number)
  • Your Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp user ID (when you message us on those platforms)
  • The content of your messages and conversations with our AI assistants
  • Business information you share (business name, type, location)
  • Appointment and scheduling information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
  • Schedule appointments and capture leads on behalf of our business clients
  • Improve and personalize our AI assistant responses
  • Send notifications to business owners about new leads and bookings
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With the business whose AI assistant you are interacting with (e.g., the plumber or real estate agent you contacted)
  • With service providers who help us operate our platform (Twilio, Meta, Google)
  • When required by law

4. Meta Platform Data

When you interact with our services through Facebook Messenger or Instagram, we receive data from Meta's platform in accordance with Meta's Platform Terms. We use this data solely to respond to your messages and provide the requested service. We do not use Meta platform data for advertising or share it with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by:

  • Emailing us at: hello@nexcomai.ai
  • Using our data deletion request page: nexcomai.ai/delete

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Conversations are encrypted in transit and stored securely. We retain conversation data for up to 90 days unless you request earlier deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
