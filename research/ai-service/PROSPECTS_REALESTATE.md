## 🔍 HOW TO FIND MORE (DIY method)

1. **Zillow** → zillow.com/professionals/real-estate-agent-reviews/clearwater-fl/
   - Filter by: solo agents, 10–50 reviews, last active < 6 months
   - These are active but not huge — perfect size

2. **Realtor.com** → realtor.com/realestateagents/clearwater_fl
   - Look for agents with personal websites (they care about their brand)

3. **Google Maps** → Search "realtor Clearwater FL" or "real estate agent Dunedin FL"
   - Under 100 reviews = small/independent
   - Check if they have a chat widget on their site — if not, that's your opening

4. **Facebook** → Search "Clearwater real estate agent" in People
   - Look for local agents posting listings
   - DM them with the demo link

## 📞 QUALIFICATION SCRIPT (Phone/DM)

**Opening:**
> "Hey [Name], I'm Hugo, based in Clearwater. I build AI assistants specifically for real estate agents — it answers buyer inquiries 24/7, qualifies leads by budget and beds, and books showings automatically. I have a live demo running right now. Mind if I send you the link? Takes 2 minutes to try."

**If interested — qualify for demo:**
Ask these 3 questions:
1. "How many leads do you typically get per week from texts/DMs?"
2. "Do you have anything that handles inquiries when you're with a client or sleeping?"
3. "Are you open to a quick 15-min Zoom to see how it works for your specific listings?"

**If they say yes to Zoom → you've got a qualified demo.**

## 🖥️ DEMO FLOW (Online or In-Person)

**Step 1 — Hook (2 min)**
> "Let me show you what your buyers would see at 11pm on a Sunday when they text about one of your listings."

- Show the bot handling: budget filter → listing results → scheduling a showing

**Step 4 — Close (3 min)**

## 📊 QUALIFICATION SCORING

Score each prospect before investing time in a full demo:

| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Solo agent or small team (not a big franchise) | +3 |
| Has personal website | +2 |
