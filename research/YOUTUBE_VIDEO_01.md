>

>

### 📊 SECTION 1 (0:45 — 2:00) — THE PROBLEM
*[On camera, conversational, maybe walk around a bit]*

> "Here's the thing nobody talks about.
>
> When you're a plumber, an electrician, a cleaning company — you're busy. You're on the job. You're driving. You're with your family at night.
>

>

> So I wanted to test this for real. I wanted to see — are local Tampa Bay businesses actually responding to customer messages after hours? Let me show you what I found."

---

### 🔍 SECTION 2 (2:00 — 4:30) — THE EXPERIMENT
*[Screen recording OR show phone screen + narrate]*

> "I picked 10 businesses at random — real local companies. I sent them all the same text message at 9:07 PM on a Tuesday.
>
> The message said:
> *'Hi, I have a leak under my kitchen sink that's getting worse. Do you have availability tomorrow? What would this cost approximately?'*
>
> That's a real customer message. That's money walking in the door.
>
> *[Show results one by one]*
>
> Business 1 — no response. Still nothing 48 hours later.
>
> Business 2 — got a response... at 8:43 the NEXT morning. 11 hours later. That customer already hired someone else.
>
> Business 3 — voicemail only. No text option.
>
> Business 4 — auto-reply: 'We'll get back to you during business hours.' That's a dead end.
>
> Business 5 — nothing.
>
> Business 6 — nothing.
>
> Business 7 — responded in 4 minutes! Amazing. And they got my business. This is the one doing it right.
>
> Businesses 8, 9, 10 — nothing. One of them responded 3 days later.
>
> *[Show graphic: 8 out of 10 didn't respond effectively]*
>
> 8 out of 10. 80% of these businesses either didn't respond at all, or responded so late the customer was long gone.
>
> And here's the math — if even ONE of those missed messages was a $400 job — that's $400 gone. Every night. Every weekend. Every time you're on another job."

## 📋 FILMING DAY CHECKLIST

**Before filming:**
- [ ] Charge phone/camera + battery pack
- [ ] Clean background visible

- [ ] Test audio — record 10 seconds and listen back

- [ ] Record each section separately (easier to edit)
- [ ] Do 2 takes of each section minimum
- [ ] Record the screen demo separately from talking head
- [ ] Leave 2 seconds of silence at start and end of each clip

- [ ] Back up all footage immediately
- [ ] Log which takes were best
- [ ] Start edit within 24 hours while it's fresh
