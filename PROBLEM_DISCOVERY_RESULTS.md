## TOP 20 PROBLEMS FOUND (Ranked by Pain + Market Size)

### TIER 1: Highest Pain + Market Size (Build These First)

#### 1. **Manual Invoicing = Cash Flow Delays** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Problem:** Small businesses manually create invoices, leading to late payments and cash flow gaps
- **Who:** Service businesses (plumbing, HVAC, electricians, cleaning, contractors)
- **Pain Level:** 9/10 (directly affects revenue)
- **Evidence:** 
  - Intuit 2025 report: 47% of small businesses cite invoicing as top automation need
  - OnPay survey: Manual invoicing is "least efficient" method
  - Multiple reports mention "invoice financing" needed due to cash flow issues
- **Potential Solution:** Auto-invoicing software that integrates with job scheduling
- **Price Willing to Pay:** $100–500/mo
- **Market Size:** 28+ million small businesses in US

#### 2. **Lose Leads While On the Job** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Problem:** Trade workers miss calls/texts while working, lose jobs to competitors
- **Who:** Plumbers, electricians, HVAC, contractors
- **Pain Level:** 9/10 (directly costs money)
- **Evidence:**
  - Reddit: Multiple plumbing threads about missing calls
  - Twilio found this exact problem (their case study)
  - Property managers post on Facebook: "Can't reach contractor"
- **Potential Solution:** AI answering service (NexcomAI does this!)
- **Price Willing to Pay:** $300–500/mo
- **Market Size:** 500k+ trade workers in US

#### 3. **Customer Scheduling Chaos** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Problem:** Managing customer appointments manually (spreadsheets, notebook, phone calls)
- **Who:** Service businesses, salons, gyms, small clinics, contractors
- **Pain Level:** 8/10 (time-wasting + double-bookings)
- **Evidence:**
  - EverCommerce 2025 report: "Dispatching and scheduling" is top pain
  - Service Fusion (competitor) raised millions on this exact problem
  - Facebook groups: "How do you manage customer appointments?"
- **Potential Solution:** Scheduling software with SMS confirmations + auto-reminders
- **Price Willing to Pay:** $200–400/mo
- **Market Size:** 5+ million service businesses

#### 4. **Late Payments Destroy Cash Flow** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Problem:** Customers don't pay invoices on time, businesses wait 30-90 days for cash
- **Who:** Service businesses, consultants, contractors, freelancers
- **Pain Level:** 8/10 (business survival issue)
- **Evidence:**
  - Intuit 2025 Late Payments Report (comprehensive)
  - Commerce Bank: "Outdated financial workflows" are the root cause
  - Multiple reports recommend automated invoice + payment reminders
- **Potential Solution:** Invoice automation + payment reminder automation
- **Price Willing to Pay:** $300–600/mo
- **Market Size:** 15+ million small businesses struggle with this

#### 5. **No Visibility Into Operations** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Problem:** Business owner doesn't know what's happening (who's working where, what jobs are done, what's pending)
- **Who:** Growing service businesses (10–50 employees), contractors
- **Pain Level:** 7/10 (inefficiency + risk)
- **Evidence:**
  - EverCommerce: "Lack of coordination" between dispatch, inventory, invoicing
  - Reddit: Small business owners saying "can't keep track"
  - Suggests need for unified dashboard/CRM
- **Potential Solution:** Operations dashboard + job tracking software
- **Price Willing to Pay:** $400–1000/mo
- **Market Size:** 3+ million mid-size service businesses
